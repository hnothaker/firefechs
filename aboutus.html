"We are artists in a new, creative and innovative generation. Fire is our paint, your eyes are our canvas."
Fusing together flow arts, dance, choreography, comedy, theater, and the elements of fire and glow, The Fire Technicians create a truly unique and face melting show of art in motion. Originally conceived in St. Louis Missouri June of 2006 with the collaboration of 5 poi artists, Fire Technicians Performance Troupe has since grown to over 20 members in three major metropolitan areas, and now features more than 10 styles of highly refined fire artistry: Poi, Double Staff, Contact Staff, Rope-Dart, Nunchaku, Hoop, Devil-Sticks, Belly Dance, Fire Breathing and Eating. Using these, the Fire Techs strive to create live and unique performance art that literally mesmerizes everyone. Demonstrating fluid, technical and choreographed performances, while still having the versatility to customize each display to suit any atmosphere. We are a passionate and talented group of friends and performers that continuously seek to break new ground and help set the pace in the global fire performance community.
~Our Mission~
Nothing brings more joy to our days than to travel to new places, meet and teach new people these arts, make and share hand made skill toys, and perform for a big crowd filled with astonished faces. Therefore our long term goal has always been to build our skills, numbers, popularity, duration and complexity of our performance to their most efficient point. By doing so we intend to afford ourselves the opportunity to turn our passion for performance into a full time gig.
